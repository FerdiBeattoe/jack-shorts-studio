# Jack SaaS Caption System v0.1

## Style

- Vertical TikTok captions.
- Big, bold, readable on mobile.
- White text with dark shadow or semi-transparent black backing.
- Punchline words may be isolated on their own frame.
- Do not over-caption every breath; caption beats.

## Safe-zone rule

Avoid bottom 300px and right 160px for TikTok UI.
Recommended caption zone:
- x: 100–980
- y: 220–1450
- max width: 880px
